Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JANUARY 2025  ·  MORDETECH SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD.

MordeTech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("MordeTech", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website at mordetech-solutions.in.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact form: Name, company, email address, phone number, project details, and budget range.
  • Careers form: Name, email, phone, work experience, LinkedIn/portfolio URL, and cover message.
  • Email & phone: When you contact us directly via email or phone.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Page views, session duration, traffic source, device type, and general geographic location (country/city level). No personally identifiable information is collected by GA4.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to remember your cookie consent preference and to enable analytics. See Section 5 for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide requested services
  • To evaluate job applications and communicate with candidates
  • To improve our website and services based on analytics
  • To send project proposals or quotes (only when explicitly requested)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Formspree: Our contact and careers forms are processed by Formspree, Inc. (formspree.io). Form submissions are transmitted securely to their servers and forwarded to our email. Their privacy policy applies.
  • Google Analytics: Anonymised usage data is shared with Google LLC. You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or government order.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact and application data for up to 2 years, or until you request deletion. Analytics data is retained per Google's standard policies (14 months by default).

5. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • mt_cookie_consent (localStorage) — Stores your cookie consent preference. Expires never (localStorage).
  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*) — Used to distinguish users and measure site usage. Only set after you Accept cookies. Expires after 2 years.

You can clear cookies at any time via your browser settings or by clearing localStorage.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at sales@mordetech-solutions.in.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. All form submissions use HTTPS encryption. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes your acceptance.
