  • Safety PLC programming (Fail-Safe F-CPUs)
  • Motion control with Sinamics drives
  • PLC-to-PLC communication (PROFIBUS, PROFINET, EtherNet/IP)
  • FAT, SAT, and commissioning support

IIoT & Edge Computing

Connect your entire factory floor to a unified data backbone. We architect IIoT systems using open standards — OPC UA, MQTT, Sparkplug B — enabling real-time monitoring from anywhere.
